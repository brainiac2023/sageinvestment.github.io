Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invest with Sage Investment ?

To make a investment you must first become a member of the platform. Once you are registered, you can make your first donation. All donations must be made through the Members Area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when register.

How do I open my Sage Investment Account?

It's quite easy and convenient. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then press "Register".

What is the minimum and maximum amount to get started?

The minimum amount is N5,000 and the maximum amount is N500,000.

How do I withdraw?

Login to your account using your username and password and check the Withdraw section.all your pending withrawals will appear on your dashboard.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password directly from your user dashboard by clicking on change password.
